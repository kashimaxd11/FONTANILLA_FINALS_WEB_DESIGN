About The Witcher IV

Learn about the next chapter in the legendary saga, currently in development

The Next Chapter

The Witcher IV marks the beginning of a new saga in the beloved fantasy universe created by Andrzej Sapkowski. Following the events of The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, this ambitious new chapter shifts focus to Ciri as she embarks on her own path as a fully-fledged witcher. Currently in development by CD Projekt RED, The Witcher IV promises to be the most immersive and ambitious open-world Witcher game to date, utilizing the power of Unreal Engine 5 to bring the Northern Kingdoms to life like never before.

Upcoming Features

New Protagonist

Experience the world through Ciri's eyes as she embraces her destiny as a witcher, combining her Elder Blood powers with traditional witcher training.

Unreal Engine 5

Built from the ground up using Epic Games' cutting-edge technology, delivering unprecedented visual fidelity and immersive gameplay.

Expanded World

Explore new regions beyond the Northern Kingdoms, encountering fresh monsters, characters, and storylines in an ever-expanding universe.

Enhanced Combat

Master a refined combat system that blends traditional witcher techniques with Ciri's unique abilities and space-time manipulation.

Deep Narrative

Immerse yourself in complex storylines with meaningful choices that shape the world around you and determine the fate of entire kingdoms.

Living Ecosystem

Encounter dynamic weather systems, day-night cycles, and a living world where your actions have lasting consequences on the environment.

Development Timeline

1

2022 - Pre-Production

CD Projekt RED announces The Witcher IV and begins pre-production work, transitioning from REDengine to Unreal Engine 5.

2

2024 - Full Production

The game enters full production phase with expanded development teams and the first gameplay systems taking shape.

3

2025 - Current Development

Active development continues with regular progress updates and the first technical demonstrations of the Unreal Engine 5 implementation.

4

2026 - Alpha Testing

Internal testing phases begin with core gameplay mechanics and world systems being refined based on feedback.

5

2027 - Launch Window

The Witcher IV is scheduled for release, marking the beginning of a new era in the beloved fantasy RPG series.

About CD Projekt RED

Founded in 2002, CD Projekt RED is an award-winning game development studio based in Warsaw, Poland. The studio is renowned for creating story-driven role-playing games that push the boundaries of interactive entertainment. Since the release of the first Witcher game in 2007, CD Projekt RED has established itself as one of the most respected RPG developers in the industry, known for their commitment to quality, player choice, and immersive storytelling.
