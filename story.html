The Story Unfolds

Discover the epic tale that awaits in The Witcher IV

A New Chapter Begins

Years have passed since the events of The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. The Northern Kingdoms are slowly recovering from the devastation of war, but new threats emerge from the shadows. Ancient prophecies speak of a coming darkness that will test the very fabric of reality itself.

In this new adventure, players will embark on a journey that spans multiple realms, where the choices made will determine not just the fate of individuals, but the destiny of entire civilizations. The School of the Wolf may be nearly extinct, but the legacy of the witchers lives on through those brave enough to take up the mantle.

The path of a witcher is never easy. We walk between worlds, neither fully human nor monster, carrying the burden of choices that others cannot make.

— Master Vesemir

The Convergence

Strange phenomena have begun manifesting across the Continent. The barriers between worlds grow thin, and creatures from other dimensions seep through the cracks in reality. These are not mere monsters to be hunted they are harbingers of something far more sinister.

The Conjunction of the Spheres, the cataclysmic event that brought monsters to this world over a millennium ago, may be happening again. But this time, the stakes are higher, and the consequences more dire than ever before.

Key Story Elements

The Elder Blood

The power of the Elder Blood continues to shape destiny. New bearers of this ancient gift must learn to control abilities that could either save or destroy the world.

The Wild Hunt's Legacy

Though defeated, the Wild Hunt's influence lingers. Their knowledge of interdimensional travel has opened doorways that should remain sealed.

The Last Schools

The few remaining witcher schools must unite in the face of this new threat. Old rivalries must be set aside to preserve what remains of their ancient traditions.

Political Intrigue

As supernatural threats grow, political powers vie for control. Kings and sorceresses play dangerous games while the world burns around them.

Zirael's story

The Witcher 4 follows Ciri as she begins her journey to become a Witcher, carving her own path after the events of The Witcher 3.

Explore a new world

Taking place in Kovir, a wealthy northern kingdom never before explored in the games. This new setting offers fresh action for worthy witchers.

Your Journey Awaits

In The Witcher IV, you will forge your own path through this rich and complex narrative. Every decision carries weight, every alliance has consequences, and every victory comes at a price. The story adapts to your choices, creating a unique experience that reflects your moral compass and strategic thinking.

Whether you choose to walk the path of the traditional witcher, embrace new magical abilities, or forge alliances with unlikely companions, your journey will be one of discovery, sacrifice, and ultimately, heroism in the face of impossible odds.
