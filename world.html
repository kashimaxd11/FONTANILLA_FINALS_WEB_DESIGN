Explore the World

Journey through the vast landscapes and mysterious realms of The Witcher universe

The Continent Awaits

The world of The Witcher IV spans across multiple kingdoms and realms, each with their own unique culture, dangers, and mysteries. From the bustling cities of the Northern Kingdoms to the wild frontiers beyond the Pontar Valley, every location tells a story of its own.

As dimensional barriers weaken and new threats emerge, familiar places have changed while entirely new regions have been discovered. The Conjunction of the Spheres has left its mark on the world, creating pockets of reality where the impossible becomes possible.

Northern Kingdoms

Novigrad

Free City

The largest city in the Northern Kingdoms, Novigrad remains a hub of trade and intrigue. Its sprawling districts hide secrets in every shadow, from the wealthy merchant quarters to the dangerous criminal underworld.

Trade Hub Eternal Fire Criminal Underworld

Oxenfurt

Academy City

Home to the famous Academy, Oxenfurt is a center of learning and knowledge. The city's libraries and scholars provide crucial insight into the supernatural phenomena plaguing the world.

Academy Libraries Scholars

Kaer Morhen

Witcher Stronghold

The ancient fortress of the School of the Wolf, now partially rebuilt. It serves as a sanctuary for the remaining witchers and a training ground for new generations of monster hunters.

Witcher School Training Grounds Ancient Fortress
